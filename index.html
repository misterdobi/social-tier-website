Features

See followers, engagement, and reach across all your platforms in one clean view. Updated in real time.

🏆

Tier Ranking System

Get ranked S through D based on your performance. See exactly what score you need to reach the next tier.

Creator Connect

Discover and follow other creators. See how you stack up against others in your niche and get inspired by top performers.

Cross-Platform Compare

Compare your TikTok, Instagram and Facebook performance side by side. Find out where your audience really lives.

How It Works

From sign up to insights
in three steps

No complicated setup. Connect your accounts and your dashboard is ready instantly.

1

Create your account

Sign up with your email or Google in seconds. No credit card required to get started.

2

Connect your social accounts

Link Instagram and Facebook via official OAuth. Your data is fetched securely — we never store your passwords.

3

Track, compare, and grow

Your dashboard populates instantly. See your tier rank, connect with creators, compare platforms, schedule posts, and export reports.
